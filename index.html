Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal? How can you offer such discounts?

Yes, it's completely legal! We work directly with official partners and purchase courses in bulk at discounted rates, similar to how large organizations buy licenses for their employees. We then pass these savings on to individual students.

Will I receive an official certificate upon completion?

Absolutely! You'll receive the exact same certificate as someone who purchased directly from Coursera or Udemy. There's no difference in the course content or credentials.

How quickly will I get access after purchasing?

Most courses are activated within 2-24 hours of purchase. We'll send you an email with login instructions as soon as your course is ready.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and in some regions, mobile payment options. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 14-day refund policy for any course that hasn't been activated yet. Once course access has been provided, standard platform refund policies apply.
